BUG 2 — iPhone Shake to Undo popup during Ballo

Problem:
On iPhone during Ballo a native popup appears:
“Annulla inserimento”
This blocks/interrupts the motion stream.
Phone bars keep moving locally sometimes, but TV bars freeze and points are not assigned.

Cause likely:
iOS Shake to Undo triggered by previous focused input / undo stack after nickname entry.

Patch:
In HomeJoin / BalloController before activating sensors:
- blur active element:
  document.activeElement?.blur?.()
- clear focus from every input/textarea/contenteditable
- set body/user container:
  user-select: none
  -webkit-user-select: none
  touch-action: manipulation
- on entering Ballo:
  temporarily disable text selection and editing focus
- after nickname submit, immediately blur input
- when Ballo starts, force blur again
- prevent any hidden input from keeping focus

Also:
- while Ballo is active, add event listeners:
  window.addEventListener('focusin', blurFocusedInput, true)
  document.addEventListener('selectionchange', clearSelection)
and remove them on unmount.

Important:
Do NOT repeatedly call DeviceMotionEvent.requestPermission.
Permission must be asked once only.

  resume motion listener
