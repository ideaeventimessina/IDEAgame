FREESTYLE BATTLE — WORD GRID VALIDATION UPDATE.

Do not touch Karaoke Live.
Do not touch Sara’Musica.
Do not touch Quizzone.
Patch only Freestyle Battle mode.

Problem:
Current Freestyle shows one word at a time.
It is too slow for real freestyle.
Players cannot wait for single-word progression.

Required new flow:

1. At start of freestyle round, generate a full word grid.
2. Show all words on TV at the same time.
3. Show the same word grid on all spectator phones.
4. Public validates each word manually by tapping it when the rapper says it.
5. At the end of timer, score = 20 points for every word confirmed by the public.

==================================================
WORD GRID
==================================================

Generate 12–20 words per freestyle round.

Each round must use different words.

Avoid repeating the exact same word set across the session.

Word categories can mix:
- objects
- emotions
- places
- party words
- absurd words
- event words
- difficult words

Example:
LUNA
FUOCO
STRADA
BICCHIERE
RUMORE
SPECCHIO
NOTTE
REGINA
CASINO
BACIO
VIAGGIO
MICROFONO

==================================================
TV UI
==================================================

TV shows:
- current rapper / team
- timer
- full word grid
- words already validated highlighted green
- score live:
  “Parole confermate: 7 / 16”
  “Punti: 140”

No one-word cycling.

==================================================
PHONE UI
==================================================

Spectator phones show:
same word grid.

Each word is a big tappable button.

When spectator hears rapper say a word:
tap the word.

After tap:
- button shows selected/confirmed locally
- disable double tap by same user for that word

==================================================
VALIDATION RULE
==================================================

A word is confirmed when:

minimum confirmations reached.

Use threshold:
max(1, ceil(activeSpectators * 0.35))

Examples:
1 spectator → 1 confirmation
3 spectators → 2 confirmations
6 spectators → 3 confirmations
10 spectators → 4 confirmations

Rapper/active performer cannot validate own words.

If no spectators:
fallback: host/TV can validate manually.

==================================================
SCORING
==================================================

At end of timer:

score = confirmedWords.length * 20

Example:
9 confirmed words → 180 points

No bonus for unconfirmed words.

Store:
validatedWords[]
wordConfirmations[wordId][playerId]

==================================================
ROUND END
==================================================

When timer expires:
- lock validation
- calculate score
- show result screen
- update ranking
- button next freestyle battle

Late taps after timer:
return/reject:
time_expired
