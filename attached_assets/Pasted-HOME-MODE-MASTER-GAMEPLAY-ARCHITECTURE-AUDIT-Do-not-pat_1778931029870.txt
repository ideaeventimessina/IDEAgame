2. COPPIE

3. SARA MUSICA
Flow:
- TV shows hint
- phones show 6 possible answers
- players compete with answer
- first correct player wins access
- TV immediately shows who answered correctly
- background music stops when chorus/audio clip starts
- chorus plays after answer validation
- background resumes after chorus

4. ADULT ONLY
Flow:
- TV shows challenge
- phones show “Accetto la sfida”
- first player who accepts appears on TV
- accepting gives 100 courage points
- other players vote 1–5 stars
- each star = 20 points
- final score = courage points + votes
- microphone/sounds/verses are phase 2, but architecture must allow it

5. SFIDA DI BALLO
Flow:
- phone must show “Attiva sensori”
- first 2 players who book enter the dance battle
- host confirms players
- host presses start
- countdown begins
- dance track starts
- TV shows both players with vertical movement meters
- phone sends sensor energy
- winner calculated automatically by movement intensity
- background music alternates with dance track

6. PAROLA ALLE SPALLE
Flow:
- player 1 books = guesser, must turn back
- player 2 books = suggester
- only after both confirmed, TV reveals word
- suggester helps guesser
- guesser presses “Rispondi” on phone and says word
- microphone/speech match validates answer
- if correct: both +100
- if wrong: suggester -50
- phone acts as controller

7. KARAOKE / FREESTYLE
When entering Karaoke room:
- host chooses Karaoke or Freestyle
Karaoke:
- player books first
- TV shows selected singer
- host confirms
- karaoke video starts
- phone microphone tracks voice
- system gives 50–200 based on pitch/match
- other players vote 1–5 stars
- each star = 20 points
Freestyle:
- beat starts
- microphone records/monitors performance
- others vote 1–5 stars
- background music alternates with game media

8. PERCORSO A RISATE
Flow:
- explanation appears on TV
- two players book
- host confirms
- challenge starts
- remaining players vote separately for each contestant
- phone is voting/controller device
- TV shows result and points
